About Rachel's Web Design

Responsible web design that aims to help spread awareness and money for environmental issues and charities alike.

I am passionate about nature, the environment and everything about it. I believe that it is not only our responsibilty to try and correct the damage that we have already done, but also to educate future generations on the dangers of our current lifestyles, and how they can change their futures to help the world.
Instead of using pushy techniques to scare and guilt people into "caring" for the environment, I prefer to nuture peoples love and passion for nature, and educate people on how they can help in simple, or drastic ways.

I design websites, games and apps that follow a theme that is educational, fun, enticing and above all spreads environmental awareness.

I also aim to make every project paper free and as environmentally friendly as possible. Where I can, I try to guide my partners about environmental issues and how they can help to support them.

Current App Projects

Please note that I have added limited details to the descriptions below for copyright reasons. If you wish to learn more about any of the projects, simply click on the project title to contact me for more details.

The Red List Educational Bundle App©
A range of fun and educational games for children up to 12 years old, teaching about endangered species across the globe. Current game ideas include: card matching game, animal maths, evolution game, word-searches, spot the difference, spelling bees and more.
Noah's Space Ark©
The world is full of rubbish left by man who have recently abandoned Earth for a new world. Can you travel the globe and find two of each animal to take to a new world aboard Noah's Space Ark©?
Interactive Animal Adoption App
Designed with Animal adoption charities in mind, this app allows the user to own a fully interactive version of their chosen animal to carry around on their phone. Users can play mini games with their adopted animal, feed, bathe and care for them, and watch them grow. With adverts to watch to help raise more funds for the charity, and direct donation links.©
