About Rachel's Web Design

Responsible web design that aims to help spread awareness and money for environmental issues and charities alike. Here at Rachel's Web Design we design websites, games and apps that follow a theme of educational, fun, enticing and above all spread environmental and animal welfare awareness. We also aim to make every project paper free and as environmentally friendly as possible. Where we can we try to educate the public and our partners about environmental issues and how they can help to support them.
We're a start up business, and we're looking for funding to help us build apps and websites that help spread awareness of environmental issues. If you are interested in investing, please send us an email at r.hovell@live.com or use the contact page to say hello, and we can get the ball rolling.

Current Projects

Please note that I have added limited details to the descriptions below for copyright reasons. If you wish to learn more about any of the projects, simply click on the project title and a new email will be created with the project title in the subject line.
The Red List Educational Bundle App©
A range of fun and educational games for children up to 12 years old, teaching about endangered species across the globe. Current game ideas include: card matching game, animal maths, evolution game, wordsearches, spot the difference, spelling bees and more.
Noah's Space Ark©
The world is full of rubbish left by man who have recently abandoned Earth for a new world. Can you travel the globe and find two of each animal to take to a new world aboard Noah's Space Ark©?
Interactive Animal Adoption App
Designed with Animal adoption charities in mind, this app allows the user to own a fully interactive version of their chosen animal to carry arround on their phone. Users can play mini games with their adopted animal, feed, bathe and care for them, and watch them grow. With adverts to watch to help raise more funds for the charity, and direct donation links.©
